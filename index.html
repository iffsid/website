Work

I'm a graduate student pursuing my doctoral degree at Purdue University under Jeffrey Mark Siskind.

My principal research interests lie in the confluence of Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Robotics, and Cognition. My research involves unifying reasoning between vision, language, and robotics; part of a long-term project to ground language in perception.

For further details, refer to the Research page.
